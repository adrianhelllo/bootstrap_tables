You can color tables, table rows, table heads or table cells with contextual colors aswell.

Class Color Meaning
.table-primary Blue Important action
.table-success Green Successful or positive action
.table-danger Red Dangerous or negative action
.table-info Light blue Neutral action or information
.table-warning Yellow Indicates a warning that might require attention
.table-active Grey Applies the hover color
.table-secondary Grey Indicates a slightly less important action
.table-light Light grey -
.table-dark Dark grey -
